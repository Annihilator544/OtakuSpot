Who are we?

We are OtakuSpot, and we're the one and only Pop Culture Club of MIT Bengaluru. Contrary to our name, we encourage and welcome every Pop Culture topic, whether it be memes or your latest Instagram trends. From anime to marvel to the wizarding world lovers, come and join us and express the Geek inside you

What do we do?

We organise themes parties, karaoke nights, movie streams and bring you a platform to discuss everything and anything Pop Culture, come get your geek on
